**题目：** Bilidown

**内容：**

哔哩哔哩视频解析下载工具，支持 8K 视频、Hi-Res 音频、杜比视界下载、批量解析，可扫码登录，常驻托盘。

Github：[https://github.com/iuroc/bilidown](https://github.com/iuroc/bilidown)

Releases：[https://github.com/iuroc/bilidown/releases](https://github.com/iuroc/bilidown/releases)

我是Widnows操作系统，下载的是`bilidown_Windows_x86_64.zip`

v2.0.15：[https://github.com/iuroc/bilidown/releases/tag/v2.0.15](https://github.com/iuroc/bilidown/releases/tag/v2.0.15)

bilidown_Windows_x86_64.zip：[https://github.com/iuroc/bilidown/releases/download/v2.0.15/bilidown_Windows_x86_64.zip](https://github.com/iuroc/bilidown/releases/download/v2.0.15/bilidown_Windows_x86_64.zip)

解压出来是如下文件：
[原图](https://f.cdn.u5md.com:8097/a69dc1abe59a5814b49293c1bb9e689b/Pasted%20image%2020260117234828.png)

如果没有弹出管理页面，可以在任务栏图标里看到进程，右键他，点击【打开主界面】
